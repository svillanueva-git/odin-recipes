Chicken Adobo

Description

The quentisential filipino dish that everyone should know how to make!

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Vinegar
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Sugar
  • Bay Leaves
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. First, marinade the chicken thighs for 20 minutes.
  2. Then sear and simmer in a pan for 25 minutes.
  3. The sauce and juices will eventually turn to glaze.
  4. Service with rice and vegetables.
